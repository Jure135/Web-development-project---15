About FIFA World Cup 26™

The biggest football event returns to the United States

Historic Moment

The FIFA World Cup 26™ marks a historic moment as the tournament returns to the United States for the first time since 1994. New York and New Jersey will host some of the most exciting matches of the tournament, including the prestigious final match.

World-Class Experience

Experience football at its finest in state-of-the-art stadiums designed to provide the ultimate fan experience. From the iconic MetLife Stadium to cutting-edge facilities, every venue is prepared to host the world's best teams and passionate fans.
